## Lists

To add unnumbered lists, you need to add items with an asteriks(*). For exemple

* lists 1
* lists 2
* lists 3

To add numbered lists, you need to add items with an ( 1 , 2 , 3 ...). For exemple

1. Lists 
2. Lists 
3. Lists
